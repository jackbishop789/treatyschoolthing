Current Implementation

Today, Treaty 1 is honored through various initiatives, including:

  • Kapyong Urban Reserve: A significant urban reserve that provides economic opportunities.
  • Long Plain First Nation Gas Station: A community-owned business supporting local economic growth.

Impact of Treaty 1

These initiatives help preserve Indigenous culture and support economic growth within the communities. They also serve as a reminder of the importance of honoring treaty commitments.
