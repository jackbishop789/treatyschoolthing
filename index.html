About Treaty 1

Treaty 1 was signed in 1871, marking an important agreement between the Crown and the Anishinaabe people. This treaty laid the groundwork for peaceful coexistence and mutual respect, recognizing Indigenous rights to land and resources.

Why Learn About Treaties?

Understanding treaties is crucial for fostering respect and recognition of Indigenous rights and cultures. It helps build relationships based on trust and collaboration.
